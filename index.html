Publish Your Google Sheet

1
Open your Google Sheet and click File → Share → Publish to web.
2
In the dialog, make sure the left dropdown shows your sheet tab (not "Entire document"), and the right dropdown is set to CSV.
3
Click Publish, confirm, then click Copy on the link that appears. It will look like:
docs.google.com/…/pub?gid=…&single=true&output=csv
4
Paste that link into the input above and click Connect. Regular share/edit links also work but require the publish step first.
5
Why Publish to web? Regular "share" links hit Google's auth flow and are blocked by browsers. The published CSV link is served without authentication and works reliably.
