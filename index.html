Publish Your Google Sheet

1
Open your Google Sheet and click File → Share → Publish to web.
2
Set the left dropdown to your sheet tab and the right to CSV.
3
Click Publish, then copy the link:
docs.google.com/…/pub?gid=…&single=true&output=csv
4
Paste that link into the input and click Connect.
5
Why Publish to web? Regular share links hit Google's auth and are blocked by browsers.
